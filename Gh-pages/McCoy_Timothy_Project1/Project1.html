Basics and explanations

Hello my eager to learn friends welcome to my beginner's HTML tutorial. I would suggest practicing this on weebly with the embed code or Custom html dragover.The first thing you ALWAYS have to add is a doctype statement by putting it in as follows: "!DOCTYPE html" when you do that you exchange the quotation marks for these: <>. Those are tags and they go around every statement. When I use quotations be sure to know that I mean these: <> Now, always put "html" right after your doctype statement on the next line and to close it you put "/html". The backslash means that it is a closing statement which is basically like the reciprocal of the first tag, thus completing the statement. In between the two html tags you made you make a line and there is where you start.
