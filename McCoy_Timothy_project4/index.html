Pokemon is a Media franchise owned by The Pokemon Company, and created by Satoshi Taijiri in 1995. The story is based around catching fictional creatures called "Pokemon," which humans then train to fight each other for sport. This franchise began as a pair of video games for the original Game boy, which was developed by Game Freak and published by Nintendo.

Ashe's Story

Ashe's character has been set as a 10 year old boy who is out on an adventure after leaving home with his new friend. This friend is his very first Pokemon and closest companion named Pikachu. Before leaving he was living in pallet town with his parents where he was going to Professor Oak's summer camp. Ashe and Pikachu's bond became so strong from an incident where Ashe was willing to risk his life to save his pikachu which sparked a strong sense of trust in pikachu.

Basic Pokemon Knowledge

In the game Pokemon there are many objectives but the most important objective out of all of them is your mission to fill up your Pokedex. The Pokedex is a device used for recording information about Pokemon that Ashe and Gary have been instructed to fill up while on their journey's. Other than trying to fill up the Pokedex you also train your Pokemon and make them stronger in order to beat gym leaders and eventually reach the (EF)

Types & Type Advantages

All Pokemon have a type and some Pokemon can be dual typed Pokemon just like how their skills can be dual typed as well. All of the known types are normal, water, electric, fighting, ground, psychic, rock, dark, steel, fire, grass, ice, poison, flying, bug, ghost, dragon, and fairy. These types hold great significance because they determine how much damage will be dealt with that specific move and how it will affect that certain Pokemon. These are more commonly known as "Type Advantages" and they help you greatly throughout the entire storyline.
